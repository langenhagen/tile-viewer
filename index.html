Currently Loaded Images:
    Usage:
    • Click "Choose Files" or press 'o' to open the file dialog. You can select and load multiple images at once.
    • Click and drag the mouse to pan.
    • Use mouse wheel to zoom.
    • Click "📄" or press 'l' to toggle the file list.
    • Press '0' to reset to the original size.
    • Click "🡄" or press '←' or 'j' to view the previous image.
    • Click "🡆" or press '→' or 'k' to view the next image.
    • Click "📋" or press 'c' or 'y' to copy the current file name to the clipboard.
    • Click "?" or press '?' to toggle the help.
